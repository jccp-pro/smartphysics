How it works: Vibration amplitude A = (apeak − amin)/2 measured over 2 s while the phone vibrates (uses current PULSE settings). Heavier loads damp the motor, reducing A. A least-squares linear fit m = k·A + b converts amplitude to mass. Calibrate with at least 2 points for best results.
